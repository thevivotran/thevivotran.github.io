About

Ground Data Collection Service

Geographic data collection software is a product of GDTS JSC, developed from a GIS platform, and can be used with smart mobile phone applications (iOS and Android) and desktop computers.

  • Step 1: Receive the data describing requested by customer

    Customers send requests and describe information to be collected in the field.

  • Step 2: Declare required data on the application (GDTS)

    GDTS will build data fields on the application based on the agreed information.

  • Step 3: Collect data on field

    Plan and operate a field data collection project for fastest results.

  • Step 4: Handing over products to customers

    Once we have collected the data, we will check the data quality and communicate with customer before handing over the product.
